ABOUT US

Shalom Stepping Stone Academy was founded in the year 2008 as a pre-school with an enrollment of 26pupil both baby and nursery class. The school went on to starting a primary school with class 1 the year 2009 with enrollment of 21pupils. The school had two teachers. The is located at Nzambani market,Muthingiini Sub-location,Nzambani location, Mtilo-Andei Division in Kibwezi Sub-country approximately 6km from Kambu town along Kambu - Komboyoo Road. The school seats on approximately 4 acres pieces of land donated by the J.C.C Church. It was registered with the Ministry of Education on August 2019. The school has formulated and developed three strategic statement i.e. School motto,vission and mission Statement.
